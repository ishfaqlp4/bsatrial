About Us

"Bangladeshi Student Association (BSA) at Southeast Missouri State University is the official organization that represents the Bangladeshi students of this university. The association aims to provide a common platform for the Bangladeshi community at SEMO. "

"It is also working relentlessly to uphold the native culture of Bangladesh. Another objective of this association is to maintain a good rapport with other cultural and ethnic groups. As a part of this, BSA organizes and participates in different cultural programs both on and off campus to represent Bangladeshi culture. BSA also works for the welfare of the society. It is taking part in different community services patronized by the university. BSA is more than a curtural group. It is a home away from home for all the Bangladeshi students at SEMO."
